Daily Quiz

What do you know about pokéballs?

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon and is perhaps the most recognizable Pokémon, serving as the franchise's mascot. It evolves from Pichu when leveled up with high friendship and can further evolve into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone. Pikachu is known for its ability to generate electricity stored in its cheeks and is often depicted as having a cheerful and playful demeanor.

Charizard is the final evolution of Charmander, evolving from Charmeleon starting at level 36. It is a Fire/Flying-type Pokémon. Charizard resembles a large, orange dragon and is known for its powerful flame attacks. In various games, it can Mega Evolve into Mega Charizard X (Fire/Dragon) or Mega Charizard Y (Fire/Flying) using Charizardite X or Charizardite Y.

Bulbasaur is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon. It evolves into Ivysaur starting at level 16, which evolves into Venusaur starting at level 32. Bulbasaur has a plant bulb on its back that grows into a large flower as it evolves. This bulb is a significant energy source for Bulbasaur, enabling it to perform various plant-based attacks.

Eevee is a Normal-type Pokémon known for its unique ability to evolve into multiple different forms, each corresponding to a different type. These evolutions include Vaporeon (Water), Jolteon (Electric), Flareon (Fire), Espeon (Psychic), Umbreon (Dark), Leafeon (Grass), Glaceon (Ice), and Sylveon (Fairy). This adaptability makes Eevee a very versatile Pokémon in battles.

Snorlax is a large, Normal-type Pokémon known for its massive size and seemingly insatiable appetite. It spends most of its time sleeping and only wakes up to eat. Despite its lazy nature, Snorlax is incredibly strong and can be a formidable opponent in battles. Snorlax is known for its high HP and strong physical attacks.
